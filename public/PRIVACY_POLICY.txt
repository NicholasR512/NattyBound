# Privacy Policy for RoverWork

Effective Date: April 2, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how RoverWork collects, uses, and protects your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect:

- Account data: Name, email, password, role
- Application data: Resume, cover letter, phone number
- Contact data: Messages submitted through contact forms
- Technical data: IP address, browser type (automatically collected)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide and maintain our services
- Match students with relevant job opportunities
- Respond to messages and requests
- Improve platform performance

We **do not sell** your personal data to third parties.

## 3. Cookies

RoverWork uses cookies for session tracking and user authentication. You may disable cookies in your browser, but this may affect functionality.

## 4. Third-Party Services

We may share information with trusted services like hosting providers or analytics tools, strictly for operational purposes.

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active or as necessary to comply with legal obligations.

## 6. Your Rights

You may:

- Request a copy of your data
- Request deletion of your account and associated data
- Contact us at [roverworka@gmail.com] for any privacy-related inquiries

## 7. Security

We implement reasonable safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission is 100% secure.

## 8. Children's Privacy

RoverWork is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this policy from time to time. Continued use of the platform constitutes acceptance of any changes.

For questions, contact us at [roverworka@gmail.com].
